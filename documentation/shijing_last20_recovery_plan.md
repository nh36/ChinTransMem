# Shijing last-20 recovery plan

This document now records the completed 2026-05-31 last-20 recovery sprint. All **305 extant _Shijing_ poems are exportable**. The only remaining metadata-only records are the six canonical title-only/lost-text entries documented in `documentation/shijing_unresolved_witness_report.md`.

## Witness search snapshot

| Route | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Internet Archive `chineseclassics41legg` hOCR/fulltext | Baseline checked | Historical blocker notes still explain why the original hOCR route was not safe enough to export these poems directly. |
| ChineseNotes Legge mirror (`alexamies/chinesenotes.com`) | Recovered/exported | Accepted as the direct working witness for the final 20 poems once provenance was recorded, raw captures were committed under `corpus/raw/chinesenotes/`, and the text passed the existing QC gates. |
| Sacred Texts / SBE Vol. III | Optional corroboration | Kept as a manual corroboration route, but not required once the ChineseNotes working-witness policy changed. |
| Chinese Text Project | Manual-assist fallback | The manual-paste workflow remains available, but it was not needed for the final recovered extant-poem set. |
| Chinese Text Initiative / UVA AnoShih | Unavailable | Historical links did not yield a usable live witness during this sprint. |
| Wengu / other Legge mirrors | Secondary cross-check | Useful for future spot checks, but not needed to finish the 305/305 extant-poem export set. |
