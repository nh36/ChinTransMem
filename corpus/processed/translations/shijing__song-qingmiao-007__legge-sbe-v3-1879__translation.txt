I have brought my offerings,
A ram and a bull.
May Heaven accept them!

I imitate and follow and observe the statutes of king Wăn,
Seeking daily to secure the tranquillity of the kingdom.
King Wăn, the Blesser,
has descended on the right, and accepted (the offerings).

Do I not, night and day,
Revere the majesty of Heaven,
Thus to preserve (its favour)?
