There is a fox, solitary and suspicious,
At that deep ford of the K'e.
My heart is sad; —
That man has no lower garment.

There is a fox, solitary and suspicious,
At that deep ford of the K'e.
My heart is sad; —
That man has no girdle.

There is a fox, solitary and suspicious,
By the side there of the K'e.
My heart is sad; —
That man has no clothes.
