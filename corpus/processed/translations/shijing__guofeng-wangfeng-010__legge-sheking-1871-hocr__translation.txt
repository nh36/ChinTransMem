1 On the mound where is the hemp,
Some one is detaining Tsze-tseay.
Some one is there detaining Tsze-tseay ; —
Would that he would come jauntily [to me] !

2 On the mound where is the wheat,
Some one is detaining Tsze-kwoh.
Some one is there detaining Tsze-kwoh ; —
Would that he would come and eat with me !

3 On the mound where are the plum trees,
Some one is detaining those youths.
Some one is there detaining those youths ; —
